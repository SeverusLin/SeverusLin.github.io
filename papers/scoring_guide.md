## How AI Scores Relevance (0–10)

- **0–2** ➤ Completely unrelated (different field)  
- **3–4** ➤ Weakly related (same broad area, but different subfield)  
- **5–6** ➤ Moderately related (shares some common concepts or techniques)  
- **7–8** ➤ Strongly related (same subfield, similar methods)  
- **9–10** ➤ Highly related (very close topic, potential overlap)

> The AI evaluates **topic**, **methodology**, and **results** to assign the score.
